    Where to stay in Milan: Top 20 kid-friendly accommodations

    By our Team of Authors

    Tips and Notes 12 mins read
    • Share this:

    Planning a family vacation in Milan? Finding the perfect place may be difficult, especially if you’re not familiar with the city. First, we will recommend the best areas of Milan, such as the Sempione district, where you will find the beautiful Parco Sempione and the iconic Castello Sforzesco.

    Also in the heart of the city is the Brera district with its many landmarks, the bustling Piazza Cadorna, Piazza Venezia with its high-end shops, and the Guastalla area with its Children's Museum.

    Now that you're a little more familiar with Milan, let's dive into this list of the top 20 family-friendly hotels and apartments in the city.

    Top 20 kid-friendly hotels and apartments

    1. Enterprise Hotel: this 4-star hotel is located in the Sempione district. It offers both suites and apartments with kitchenettes, suitable for larger families. It’s pet-friendly and accessible.
    2. Hotel Ambasciatori: this 4-star hotel is conveniently close to the Duomo. It’s accessible and pet-friendly. It offers spacious family rooms and a laundry service.
    3. Hotel Petit Palais: this 4-star hotel is located in a 17th century building and is pet-friendly. Some of the rooms feature a kitchenette.
    4. Hotel Novotel Milano Nord Ca’ Granda: this hotel is conveniently close to the Ca’ Granda metro station. It features a terrace and an outdoor pool. Children under 15 years old stay for free.
    5. Innside by Melià: this 4-star hotel is close to the Milano Centrale station. It’s accessible and pet-friendly. It offers luxury apartments with a panoramic view of the city.

    6. Ramada Plaza by Wyndham: this 4-star hotel is close to the Turro metro station. It offers an outdoor pool and a fitness center. Children under 17 years old stay for free.
    7. Chateau Monfort: this 5-star hotel is perfect for a luxury vacation. It’s pet-friendly and accessible. It offers an indoor pool and a spa. Children under 12 stay for free.
    8. Hotel Milano City Center Duomo: this 3-star hotel is close to the Castello Sforzesco and the Duomo. It offers triple rooms and it’s pet-friendly.
    9. Hotel Dover: this 2-star hotel is perfect if you want to travel on a budget without staying too far away from the city center. It offers triple rooms and it’s pet-friendly.
    10. Sant’Ambrogio Apartments: these apartments are located in the heart of the city. They include family apartments with a full kitchen.

    Accommodation in the city center of Milan is more expensive than any other city in Italy. You might want to consider staying just out of it but next to a metro station so that you can easily reach downtown quickly enough.

    11. Novotel Milano Linate Aeroporto: this modern 4-star hotel is close to the Linate Airport, making it perfect for tourists. It includes a play area for children, a garden and an international restaurants. Children under 16 years old stay for free.
    12. Grand Visconti Palace Hotel: this 4-star hotel is close to the Duomo. It offers family rooms and features a spa and a fitness center, as well as an indoor pool.
    13. Moscova Luxury B&B Milano: this B&B is located near the metro station and offers both suites and apartments, some of which include kitchenettes.
    14. Just Hotel: this 4-star hotel is located near the metro line. It offers both triple and quadruple rooms, making it perfect for larger families.
    15. Radisson Blu Hotel: this 4-star hotel features an indoor swimming pool a fitness center. It’s also pet-friendly.

    16. Hotel Nasco: this 4-star hotel offers triple rooms and apartments with kitchenettes. It’s accessible and pet-friendly.
    17. IQ Hotel Milano: this 4-star hotel is conveniently close to the Milano Centrale station. It offers a rooftop bar, a spa and a gym. It’s perfect for large families with its triple and quadruple rooms.
    18. Milano Verticale: this luxurious 4-star hotel is accessible and pet-friendly. It offers family suites with numerous services and an incredible view of the city.
    19. Galleria Vik Milano Hotel: this 5-star hotel is perfect for a luxury vacation for your family. It offers various spacious rooms and it’s located in the heart of the city.
    20. Hotel Avani Palazzo Moscova Milan: this 4-star hotel is pet-friendly and accessible. It has a spa and a fitness center and it offers a laundry service.
