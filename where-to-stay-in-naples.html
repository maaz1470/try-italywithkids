    Where to stay in Naples: Top 20 kid-friendly accommodations

    By our Team of Authors

    Tips and Notes 8 mins read
    • Share this:

    If you're planning a family vacation in Naples, the first thing you need to know is which areas of the city are best for you.

    Naples is divided into many different neighborhoods, but only some of them are suitable for a nice vacation. These include Chiaia, an upscale neighborhood on the seafront that is home to several important landmarks such as Castel Dell'Ovo.

    The city center is also perfect if you want to be close to the city's highlights, while the Vomero district is located on a hill overlooking Naples and it's away from the busier areas.

    Top 20 kid-friendly hotels and apartments

    Without further ado, here’s our list of the top 20 family-friendly hotels and apartments of the city:

    1. Hotel Nunù: this 4-star hotel is located on a bustling road lined up with restaurants and shops. It has soundproofed rooms and the concierge will help you organize trips in the city or in the nearby areas.
    2. Ecumano Space: this 3-star hotel is located near the city center and it features an indoor pool. It’s both family-friendly and pet-friendly.
    3. B&B Hotel Napoli: this 3-star hotel offers both triple and quadruple rooms, making it perfect for large families. It’s pet-friendly and accessible.
    4. Relais Della Porta: this beautiful luxury hotel is located near the Quartieri Spagnoli district, in a 16th century restored building. The concierge will organize activities for you and your families, such as tours of the city.
    5. Starhotel Terminus: this 4-star hotel is perfect if you want to remain close to Napoli Centrale train station. It offers both family rooms and connecting rooms, and it’s pet-friendly.

    6. Maison Arena Bovio: this hotel is located near the Università metro station and it’s perfect for families who want to travel on a budget. It offers spacious family rooms and it’s not far from many important highlights of the city.
    7. Palazzo Alabardieri: this 4-star hotel is located close to the beautiful Villa Comunale di Napoli. It offers both triple and quadruple rooms, and it’s pet-friendly.
    8. Best Western Hotel Plaza: this 4-star hotel is located near the Napoli Centrale station and it’s accessible. It offers a laundry service and it’s family-friendly.
    9. Partenope Relais: this 4-star hotel is located at the seafront and it features beautiful luxury suites. It’s pet-friendly and accessible.
    10. The Britannique Naples: this beautiful 5-star hotel is located in a 19th century villa and has a terrace facing the sea as well as a spa. It’s accessible and pet-friendly.

    If suggest to spend at least two full days in Naples, because the city has got a lot to offer and from there you can take great day trips to Pompeii, Vesuvius and Amalfi Coast

    11. Hotel San Francesco al Monte: this 4-star hotel is located in a 16th century monastery near the Quartieri Spagnoli area. It features a garden, a spa and an outdoor pool. Its rooms can host up to 3 adults plus 1 child.
    12. Covo degli Angioini: this 3-star hotel is located near Castel Nuovo and offers both rooms and apartments, making it perfect for larger families. It’s pet-friendly and some of the apartments feature a kitchenette.
    13. Hotel Tiempo: this 3-star hotel offers triple and quadruple rooms. The services of this hotel include family excursions organized by the concierge, babysitting and dogsitting.
    14. Grand Hotel Santa Lucia: this luxury 4-star hotel is close to Castel dell’Ovo and offers family room and babysitting service.
    15. B&B I Borbone Napoli: this 3-star hotel is located in the heart of the city. It offers family rooms and it’s pet-friendly. The concierge can organize tours of Naples for you and your family.

    16. Superotium Hotel: this hotel is located in the central area of Naples. Its rooms can host up to 5 people and it’s pet-friendly.
    17. Hotel Cineholiday: this 3-star hotel is perfect if you want to remain close to the Napoli Centrale station. It’s pet-friendly and has a private parking.
    18.Grand Hotel Europa: this 3-star hotel is located near the train station. It offers triple, quadruple and family rooms as well as a variety of services and activities. It’s accessible and pet-friendly.
    19. Ramada by Wyndham Hotel: this 4-star hotel offers babysitting services and cots, as well as family and oversized rooms for large families.
    20. Hotel Colombo: this 3-star hotel offers triple and quadruple rooms. It’s perfect if you want to travel on a budget, as kids under 2 years old stay for free.
